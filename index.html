About Us
Middle East Oilfield General Supplies (MOGS) is a professional trader for lubricants, pipes, tubes fittings & Valves with experience +10 years in Egypt and UAE.
We are supplying for Oil, Gas, Petrochemical, and Power Generation industries . Head quarter located in Abu-Dhabi UAE , Our branch in Egypt with two warehouses (Suez Warehouse 3,000 M2) & (Cairo Warehouse 1,000 M2).
Our sources from USA, UK, Italy, South Africa, China, and India .
